I am Gayathri Balaji

Welcome to my DiaryVerse

where my thoughts, emotions and feelings
come to die only to be reborn as verse!

They say the eyes never lie.
Every colour, every shape, every flutter of a lash,
And every soul behind it, carries a story.
Only some witness the struggles,
Only few see the pain,
And only YOU know the WHOLE.
The whole story lives in my eyes.
Can you read mine?

About the Author

Hmm… About me. What do I tell about myself? Well, for starters, I’m the kind of person who refuses to sit idle—mostly because my brain likes to remind me that my bucket list is roughly the size of the night sky. So instead of doing nothing, I do the next best thing: I overthink with full commitment.
I’m a working professional who also happens to be an author and poet, an artist who sketches with surprising competence, and a budding flutist. At work, I’m the unofficial, unpaid meme engine that keeps my colleagues entertained. Outside work, I’m a wanderer at heart, a self‑proclaimed vagabond, and a photographer with a very specific niche: candid photos of still things. Yes, it’s a thing. Yes, I made it a thing. I think that’s enough flexing for the introduction, let's keep the rest of my talents hidden.
